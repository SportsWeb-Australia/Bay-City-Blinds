# Bay City Blinds Business One Integration Map

## Goal

The website must connect cleanly into the Business One platform and support lead capture, CRM follow-up, automation, reporting, and future client service workflows.

## Main Lead Capture

Primary form:
Free Measure and Quote

Suggested fields:
- First name
- Last name
- Phone
- Email
- Suburb
- Service interest
- Preferred contact method
- Best time to contact
- Timeframe
- Message
- Consent checkbox
- Lead source
- Campaign source

## Service Interest Options

- Blinds
- Curtains
- Shutters
- Outdoor blinds
- Not sure / need advice
- Other

## Workflow

On form submission:
1. Create lead/contact in Zoho CRM or Business One CRM.
2. Store service interest.
3. Store suburb.
4. Store lead source.
5. Send internal notification.
6. Send customer confirmation email.
7. Create follow-up task.
8. Add to appropriate pipeline stage.
9. Trigger SMS/email follow-up if enabled.
10. Track conversion in analytics.

## CTA Tracking

Track:
- free quote clicks
- phone clicks
- email clicks
- form starts
- form submissions
- booking clicks
- gallery interactions if useful
- service page CTA clicks
