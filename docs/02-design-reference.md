# Bay City Blinds Design Reference

## Design Goal

The website should look like a premium local home improvement brand, not a cheap trade website and not a generic AI-generated landing page.

## Visual Style

Bright, airy, clean, polished, warm, practical, and professional.

Use strong imagery, generous whitespace, clear typography, trust sections, and confident calls to action.

## Inspiration

Draw broad design inspiration from:
- Wynstan
- Hillarys
- Shutterly Fabulous

Do not copy these sites. Use them only for quality, structure, confidence, product presentation, and conversion flow.

## What To Preserve

- Clean professional layout
- Strong first screen
- Clear free quote CTA
- Local trust
- Product/service clarity
- Human story
- Mobile-first experience
- Bright home/lifestyle feel

## What To Avoid

- Generic AI-looking gradients
- Random decorative blobs
- Overly dark layouts
- Weak CTAs
- Cluttered product cards
- Too many icons
- Fake SaaS-style sections
- Overly corporate language
- Long walls of text
- Design drift from the approved direction

## Hero Requirements

The hero must clearly explain:
- who Bay City Blinds helps
- what they provide
- where they work
- why to trust them
- how to take action

Primary CTA:
Request a Free Measure & Quote

Secondary CTA:
View Products or Call Now
