# Bay City Blinds Website Brief

## Project Goal

Build a premium Astro website for Bay City Blinds that generates leads for blinds, curtains, shutters, and related window furnishing services in Geelong and surrounding areas.

The website must make it easy for visitors to request a free measure and quote.

## Primary Conversion

Free measure and quote enquiry.

## Secondary Conversions

- Phone call
- Email enquiry
- Booking request
- Product/service enquiry
- Gallery/review engagement
- Newsletter or remarketing capture if appropriate

## Core Positioning

Bay City Blinds is a local Geelong blinds business offering personal service, practical advice, quality products, and convenient measure-and-quote support.

The website should sell the advantage of:
- local owner/operator service
- mobile showroom convenience
- expert installation
- genuine advice
- real people
- strong reviews
- easy process

## Key People

Jackson:
Owner and face of the business.

Tim:
Carpenter/installer and part of the trust story.

The site should use the people behind the business as a strength.

## Important Messages

- Free measure and quote
- Mobile showroom
- Local Geelong service
- Quality blinds, curtains, shutters, and outdoor options
- Friendly expert advice
- Professional installation
- Easy process from consultation to installation
- Trusted local service

## Suggested Pages

- Home
- Blinds
- Curtains
- Shutters
- Outdoor Blinds
- About
- Reviews
- Gallery
- Service Areas
- Contact / Free Measure and Quote

Optional SEO pages:
- Blinds Geelong
- Curtains Geelong
- Shutters Geelong
- Outdoor Blinds Geelong
- Suburb pages if justified

## Homepage Section Order

1. Hero with free measure and quote CTA
2. Trust bar / local proof
3. Product/service cards
4. Mobile showroom / how it works
5. Meet Jackson and Tim
6. Reviews/testimonials
7. Gallery or inspiration section
8. Service areas
9. FAQs
10. Final CTA

## Tone

Professional, local, confident, warm, practical, trustworthy.
